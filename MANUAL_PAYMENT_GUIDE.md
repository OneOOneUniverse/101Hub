# Manual Payment Guide for 101Hub Customers

## 📱 How to Make Your Manual Payment

### Step 1: Open Your Mobile Money or Banking App
- **MTN Mobile Money** (dial `*170#`)
- **Vodafone Cash** (dial `*110*110#`)
- Your bank's mobile app
- Any other digital payment service you use

---

### Step 2: Send the Transfer
**Recipient Phone Number:**
```
+233 548656980
```

**Amount to Send:**
```
Check your order email for the exact downpayment amount (40% of total)
```

**Example:**
- If order total is GHS 500
- Send GHS 200 (40% downpayment)
- Pay remaining GHS 300 at delivery

---

### Step 3: Wait for Confirmation
Your app will show a **confirmation screen** with:
- ✓ Recipient name or number
- ✓ Amount sent
- ✓ Transaction reference number
- ✓ Date and time
- ✓ Status (usually "Success" or "Completed")

---

### Step 4: Take a Screenshot
📸 **This is the most important step!**

Take a clear screenshot that clearly shows:
1. **Recipient phone number** → `+233 548656980`
2. **Amount sent** → The exact amount from your order
3. **Transaction reference** → The reference/confirmation number
4. **Date & Time** → When the transaction was made
5. **Status** → Confirmation of successful payment

**Example of what should be visible:**
```
From: MTN Mobile Money
To: +233 548656980
Amount: GHS 200.00
Reference: TX123456789
Status: ✓ Successful
Date: 10 April 2026, 2:45 PM
```

---

### Step 5: Upload the Screenshot
Go back to 101Hub and upload the screenshot in the **"Upload Payment Proof"** section during checkout.

**File requirements:**
- Image formats: JPG, PNG, WebP (etc.)
- Max size: 5MB
- Must be clear and readable

---

### ❌ What NOT to Do

| ❌ Don't | Why |
|---------|-----|
| Send from someone else's account | We can't verify it's from you |
| Upload a blurry screenshot | Impossible to verify |
| Screenshot without amount visible | We can't confirm the right amount paid |
| Screenshot without date/time | We can't match it to your order |
| Forget to include reference number | Makes verification harder |

### 📞 Need Help?

**Can't find your payment confirmation?**
- Check your app's transaction history
- Look for "Recent Transfers" or "Payment History"
- The confirmation usually appears within 10 seconds

**Screenshot not uploading?**
- Make sure file is smaller than 5MB
- Try a different image format
- Clear browser cache and try again

**Still having issues?**
- Call/WhatsApp: **+233 548656980**
- Email full details of your order reference
